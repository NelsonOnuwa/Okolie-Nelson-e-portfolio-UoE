Learning Outcomes

I inquired into the diversity of different data types, data sources, data storage format and methods of data collection.

Next, I explored the concepts of big data, big data security, big data security challenges and how to handle big data security challenges.

Later on, I grew my comprehension of the process of data wrangling and the challenges faced during data wrangling. I discovered how to use data wrangling to eliminate duplications and redundancies, thereby improving the quality of data collected in the raw state to a more usable format through cleansing and normalization.

I am now well acquainted with the methodologies and tools in data wrangling.

    Our Team performed a data business analysis on an Airbnb dataset in New York City

    We came up with business-oriented questions relevant to Airbnb.

    We performed exploratory data analysis (EDA) on the Airbnb dataset.

    Using machine learning algorithm, we applied regression models for pricing prediction and clustering techniques for customer segmentation.

    Through active participation in our team activities, meetings and collaboration, I was able to develop soft skills such as communication and listening skills, critical thinking skills, problem solving skills, collaboration skills and team player skills.

    I also acquired the skills required to be an effective member of a development team in a virtual professional environment.
